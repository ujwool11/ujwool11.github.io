About Us

UJ Wool is a love story woven stitch by stitch, born from a shared passion for slow craft and thoughtful design. Based in New Delhi, our handmade crochet creations celebrate the beauty of patience, warmth, and individuality in a world that moves too fast.

Every piece begins as a simple strand of yarn and transforms, by hand, into something meaningful—made not just to be worn or used, but to be felt.

At UJ Wool, we believe handmade carries a soul. Each crochet item is carefully crafted with attention to detail, inspired by everyday moments, cozy textures, and timeless patterns. No two pieces are ever exactly the same, and that’s the magic—we embrace imperfections as proof of human touch, creativity, and care.

More than a business, UJ Wool is our small dream stitched into reality. Rooted in New Delhi and reaching hearts beyond, we create with intention, warmth, and gratitude—hoping our handmade crochet brings comfort, charm, and a little bit of joy into your everyday life 🧶✨

🔮❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🧶 Are all your products 100% handmade? +
Yes! Every single item at UJ Wool is lovingly handcrafted right here in New Delhi. No machines, no mass production — just pure handmade crochet made with premium quality wool and lots of care. That's why no two pieces are ever exactly alike. The tiny imperfections you sometimes spot are proof of real human hands at work!
🚚 How long does delivery take, and what are the shipping charges? +
We typically dispatch orders within 2–3 business days of payment confirmation. Delivery across India generally takes 5–8 business days depending on your location. Shipping charges are calculated on actuals for orders below ₹1000 and will need to be paid separately post successful delivery and customer satisfaction. For orders above ₹1000, we offer FREE delivery right to your doorstep! 🎁
🎨 Can I place a customized / personalized order? +
Absolutely — customized orders are one of our specialities! You can choose your preferred colors, sizes, and patterns for most of our products listed under the Customized Section above. Browse the Customized Products section on our homepage and add what catches your eye to your cart. If you have a very specific request, feel free to reach out to us directly on WhatsApp or Instagram and we'll do our best to bring your vision to life. 💕
💳 What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept payments via all major UPI apps including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and any other UPI-enabled application. For your convenience, we also support online card payments through our secure Razorpay payment gateway. All payments are processed securely and we do not store any payment details on our side. After payment, please keep your transaction ID / UTR number handy for order confirmation.
🔁 What is your return / exchange policy? +
Since every product is handmade-to-order, we currently do not offer returns for change of mind. However, if your order arrives damaged, defective, or significantly different from what was shown, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos via WhatsApp or Instagram. We'll do our best to make it right — either through an exchange or a resolution that works for you. Your satisfaction is our priority! 🧶✨
